September 22, 2009
Welcome!

Bug Fixes
1. Modified the More Info button to more closely reflect what it does - it is now called the Expand button. Also, while the display is expanded and you are in an area that the Expand button is not an option, the display returns to normal.
2. Correctly aligned the days and hours in the Hours area.
3. The disappearing scrollbar has returned for all areas. The scrollbar now appears when it is needed.
4. The suggested articles in the My Library area now includes news and articles written today.
5. Catalog links found in the text of news, articles and events should now open the link in the dockable frame as intended.

New Features
1. The list of required materials for the Black & White Masquerade now has data.

Other
1. Adjusted the display area to show less data when the screen is smaller than 1024 x 768.

Known Issues
1. Links with & and " may not all be working. These special characters sometimes appear in the titles of news, articles, events and materials.
2. The Write A Review link in the Materials area is not working. It has been removed and it should return, fully functional, in the next patch.

September 17, 2009
Welcome!

Bug Fixes
1. Improved site navigation.
2. Reworked the client to make the site both load faster and work better in Internet Explorer.
3. Greatly improved support for Internet Explorer 7 and 8.
4. Links in articles are now easier to see and use.

New Features
1. Added many new themes, all of which can be found under Options.
2. Events at your home library now show on the My Library page.
3. Recent news and articles now show on the My Library page.
4. Update notes are now visible by clicking on Most Recent Update from the My Library page
5. There are now RSS feeds for News, Events, and Materials. Also, individual writers have their own RSS feed.
6. You can now filter News by Favorite.
7. You can now filter Events by Favorite, Anticipated, Summer Reading Program and Card Slider.
8. You can now filter Materials by Favorite, Watch List and Collection.
9. Date selector has been vastly improved. You can now select a date from a different month. This feature is on News and Events.
10. It is now easier and faster to search the catalog. Any link that has a cat icon next to it will take you directly to material in the catalog.
11. There are now icons throughout the site to aid in navagation. The icons and the links with these icons will take you to that area of LAPCAT.

Known Issues
1. In Internet Explorer and on the Create An Account sign-up page, the security-word box (CAPTCHA) has issues.
